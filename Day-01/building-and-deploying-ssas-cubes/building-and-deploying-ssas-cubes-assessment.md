## Section B: Scenario Question (3 points)

**Scenario:**

The cube deploys successfully but business users see empty results in the browser. Explain the most likely cause and the validation sequence you would follow.

**Your answer should include:**

- A clear explanation of the SSAS concept involved
- How it connects to the Assmang business context
- What you would recommend and why
- Any risks or considerations

## Section C: Practical Challenge (5 points)

**Task:**

Build, deploy, process, and browse the Assmang cube. Capture evidence that revenue and costs are visible by mine and by month.

**Submission requirements:**
